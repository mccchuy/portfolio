Pastelitos is a web-design project created for the CS 20 course midterm at Tufts. It is designed to display a theoretical small, minority-owned pastry shop in Boston. It features a home page, menu, order form, sign in/sign up page, and contact page.

Ideation and Prototyping

>

The objective of this project was to simply create a website for a business or an organization/cause that relies on its supporters. After discussing amongst the four of us, we quickly realized that we had a common interest: baking. We all shared core memories of baking and cooking with family, and wanted to create a website that would reflect that. With that, we started prototyping possible themes for our site. Ultimately, we decided on the theme of the top right to implement our site with.

Visual Identity

Creation

In creating our site, we wanted to incorporate a visual identity of bright, eye-catching colors, mouthwatering pastries, and an easy, intuitive user experience. To accomplish this, we drew upon other pastry shop sites in our research and decided we needed the following:

  • An eye-catching homepage
  • An accessible navigation bar
  • A menu that reflected the largely Latinx identity of the team
  • A contact page for comments, questions, and concerns
  • A cake order form
  • A signup/sign in page for continutity of usage
